About Me

I lead the Sora team as a VP of Research at OpenAI. Our team works on building the next generation of video generation models and developing new experiences that will be enabled by them.

Previously, I created DALL·E in 2021, the first text-to-image generation model with the ability to combine a wide range of concepts from language in plausible ways, a step toward machines being able to manipulate visual concepts with human-like flexibility. In 2022, I co-created DALL·E 2, the first text-to-image generation model to be deployed in production, introducing inpainting, variations, and blending – features now widely used in image generation products. Subsequently, I led the team that created DALL·E 3, deployed in ChatGPT in 2023, advancing the state of the art in enabling image generation models to respond faithfully to user instructions. This team has since evolved to become what the Sora team is today.
